    Aptitude Tests

    Aptitude tests are used by the employers to assess you, in terms of your skills, knowledge, ability and personality. Aptitude tests are an accurate way of predicting the potential contribution of a candidate and the likelihood of them achieving success within the organization.

    Mock Interviews

    A mock interview, is a simulation of an actual job interview. It provides you with an opportunity to practice for an interview and receive feedback on your interview skills.
